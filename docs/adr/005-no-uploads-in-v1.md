# ADR 005: Exclude document uploads from version 1

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-04-14

## Context

The product’s version 1 goal is triage and next-step guidance, not case-file collection. Uploads would add technical, safeguarding, and retention complexity.

## Decision

Version 1 will ask about evidence categories but will not collect document uploads.

## Consequences

- the service remains lighter, safer, and easier to deploy
- evidence readiness is captured through structured questions
- any future upload flow would require separate policy, retention, and safeguarding decisions

## Review trigger

Review if partner operations show that triage quality is materially blocked by the absence of uploads.
